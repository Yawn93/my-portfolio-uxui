Brussels Garden

UX/UI DESIGN

Description

This is a schedule app for a gardener in Brussels. In this project, we focused on researching the area and demographics, build the personas and validate their needs through updating wireframes accordingly. Brussels Garden wants to have good usability and be inclusive by maintaining a simple process.

Goals

In perfecting the Brussels Garden schedule app for our project, we carefully considered user feedback, making the app more user-friendly. Regular updates ensure it stays intuitive and caters to the changing needs of the gardening community. Our commitment is to create a responsive and user-centric design for a smooth scheduling experience in this conceptual project.
