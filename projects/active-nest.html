Active Nest

UX/UI DESIGN

Description

This project introduces an AI-powered fitness app designed for individuals who prioritize convenience, affordability, and a personalized workout experience. Whether users are short on time, lack access to a gym, or prefer exercising at home, this app transforms any space into a dynamic fitness environment.

Goals

This app redefines at-home fitness by making it accessible, engaging, and results-driven, ensuring every user feels supported and motivated throughout their journey.
