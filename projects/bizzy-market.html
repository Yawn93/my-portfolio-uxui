Analysis

To understand the needs of both local producers and consumers, we conducted a comprehensive research phase. This included questionnaires to gather direct feedback from potential users, free research on e-commerce trends and local product markets, and an in-depth study of sustainability practices. The insights gained from this research helped us identify key pain points, such as visibility challenges for small businesses and the demand for an intuitive shopping experience. These findings served as the foundation for defining our personas and shaping the core functionalities of the app.



Personas

Based on our research, we identified key user groups for the app: local producers seeking better visibility and sales opportunities, and conscious consumers looking for authentic, high-quality Portuguese products. Understanding their needs, challenges, and behaviors helped shape the app‚Äôs features, ensuring an engaging experience for both sides.
